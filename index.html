About Us

The Northwestern University Student Chapter of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers is an organization dedicated to providing the resources and outlets through which the University's aspiring engineers, tinkerers, and hackers can learn and create with their craft.

What We Do

Educate

We have an AE Program and provide advising on EECS classes before registration each quarter.

Exposure

We host companies on campus to give tech talks.

Enjoy

We host EECS-related events, such as the much-anticipated EECS Olympics and always-popular EECS Barbeque. We also take a quarterly trip to Yeowoosai.
