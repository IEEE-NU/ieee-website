What Is It?

Interested in exploring the world of software development? Want to take a crack at building something fun or useful with your hardware of choice? Eager to start some sort of project, but don't know how to get going?

Every fall quarter, the IEEE Executive Board takes motivated students and guides them through a program focused on mentorship and the introductory exploration of an EECS-related technology.

Project Showcase

At the end of winter quarter, the teams who feel confident in their project are given with the oppurtunity to present their product in front of a panel of judges. This event is openn to the public, and fellow students as well as anyone in the area can come by and take a look at what our members have built.

This is not simply limited to the IE3 program. Any person or group who wishes to can participate and compete in the showcase.
